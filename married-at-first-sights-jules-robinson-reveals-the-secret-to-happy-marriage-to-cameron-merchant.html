Married at First Sight’s Jules Robinson reveals the secret to her happy real-life marriage to Cameron Merchant – as she reflects on turning 40 after amazing 20 kilo weight loss

By Marta Jary For Daily Mail Australia

Published: 07:19 EST, 5 March 2022 | Updated: 08:21 EST, 5 March 2022


They are one of Married at First Sight’s greatest success stories.

And in this week’s Stellar Magazine, Jules Robinson, 39, revealed the secrets to her happy marriage with Cameron Merchant, 38. 

‘We’re just like every couple. We bicker about ridiculous things,’ she told the publication. 

Happy marriage: In this week’s Stellar Magazine, Jules Robinson (pictured) revealed the secrets to her happy marriage with Cameron Merchant

‘But one thing that’s constant is that we care about our relationship and each other. We give a s**t about each other’s dreams, passions and interests.’ 

Elsewhere in the interview, Jules reflected on her upcoming 40th birthday as she prepares to reach the milestone this month. 

‘I truly feel like I’ve found my confidence and my groove. I’m owning who I am and just embracing being happy,’ she said.

Jules revealed in October she had nearly lost ’20 kilograms’ after signing on as the face of weight loss company, Jenny Craig. 

She claims that 20 kilos had ‘crept on’ her figure after she had welcomed her son Ollie.
