Celebrity pets can sometimes be more famous than the celebrities themselves.

Fans are all over social media, trying to learn as much as possible about their favorite stars. Getting to see a glimpse of their personal lives is a thrill to so many fans and followers, and many of the world's most famous faces are happy to oblige. Among the posts that generate a whole lot of interest are those of celebrity pets that have now become as famous as their celebrity owners. The love that these celebrities have for their animals is broadcast all over their social media pages and acts as an instant way for them to connect with their fans on a personal level. Of course, fans lap up all the furry-friend-love that these stars have to offer and have established quite a relationship with these very spoiled pets that are truly living in the lap of luxury. Elle reports that some of these animals have gained a lot of traction online, and it's easy to see why.

Kylie Jenner

With well over 217 million followers keeping tabs on her every move, Kylie Jenner made her adorable puppy, Kevin, an instant star the moment that she posted his photo.

Well over 5 million people 'liked' the first photo of him that she shared. This led to some adorable snaps that captured tender moments between Stormi and Kevin that left fans cooing in awe of their cuteness. Jenner has referenced Kevin as being 'her son,' and he has a strong social media following already!

Camila Cabello

Fans were already mesmerized by the bond that Camila Cabello and Shawn Mendes shared with one another and were thrilled to see this couple take their bond to the next level by adopting a new puppy named Tarzan. They've referred to him as their 'sweet miracle' and have enjoyed lounging with him during their forced downtime, resulting from the pandemic.

Ariana Grande

Ariana Grande has an absolutely adorable beagle-chihuahua pup named Toulouse, who has graced her social media so many times that he's become a household name among fans. It's not just Toulouse that has stolen the hearts of her fans, though. She has a total of 9 dogs that she frequently boasts about, and it's clear she's absolutely smitten by each of them.

Paris Hilton

Paris Hilton is obsessed with her tiny pups. She has so many pint-sized dogs that fans have a hard time keeping track of them all. Recently featured on her Instagram account are her dogs Dollar, Prada, Slivington, Marilyn Monroe, Prince Hilton, Princess Paris Jr., Peter Pan, Diamond Baby, and Tinkerbell.

The dogs owned by Hilton have polarized fans who can't believe they have their very own dog mansions complete with air conditioning and their very own furniture. Paris Hilton obsessively spends hundreds of thousands of dollars to ensure that her dogs live in the lap of luxury, inclusive of dressing them in designer clothing. One of them even has a Hermes mini-purse strapped on to its back.

Selena Gomez

Selena Gomez has a very special bond with Winnie, and fans have watched as she snuggles up to her dog for love and support during her most difficult days. Winnie has a tender, loving personality, and millions of fans 'like' her images each time she makes an appearance with her pet-mama.

Jennifer Aniston

Clyde is the cute terrier that belongs to Jennifer Aniston, and she spends a whole lot of time fussing over her beloved pet and sharing images of him on Instagram for her fans to enjoy. Her fans have gotten to know Clyde through the numerous posts Aniston shares of him online, and the little fellow has touched the lives of fans across the globe.

Nick Jonas & Priyanka Chopra

Priyanka Chopra introduced her German Shepherd pup to the world at the very same time that she introduced him to her husband, Nick Jonas. She gifted Gino to Nick in celebration of their one-year anniversary, and fans were in awe of the tender moment which they were able to view and be a part of. The moment she put Gino into bed with Nick, who was asleep at the time, it became clear that Gino would steal the hearts of millions of fans.

Taylor Swift

Taylor Swift is one of the proudest cat mamas that social media has ever seen. Her heart is so full of love for her adorable cats, and she frequently shares their photos and talks about the impact that they've had on her life. Meredith Grey and Olivia Benson are named after characters on Grey's Anatomy and Law & Order SVU and are as famous as their mama is. They're even part of her video shoots!

Victoria Beckham

Victoria Beckham's dog is well known by fans that have enjoyed getting to know him through her Instagram feed. Fig has regularly appeared on the singer's social media and has been her saving grace during the pandemic. Millions of Beckham's followers affectionately recognize Fig as being a critically important member of the Beckham family.

Kaia Gerber

Cindy Crawford's very famous daughter, Kaia Gerber, has fostered a puppy from La Belle Foundation, an organization many celebrities have tapped into in search of their furry pals. She has grown so fond of her pup that she's been blasted on social media, and fans expect to see Milo cuddled with Kaia more often than not!

Cara Delevingne

More gifts of furry love can be credited to the La Belle Foundation, as Cara Delevingne celebrated the addition of Bowie and Hendrix into her life. She's often depicted on social media kissing her pups, and in spite of the fact that they're her foster puppies, they've already made quite a name for themselves on social media, and fans hope she decides to keep them!

Dua Lipa And Anwar Hadid

Dexter is officially Insta-famous! This little cutie belongs to Dua Lip and Anwar Hadid, and their love for him has catapulted this puppy to extreme levels of fame already. Millions of people 'like' the photos that he appears in, and fans are really hoping he'll get his very own social media account one day.
