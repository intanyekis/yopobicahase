Gigi Hadid And Bradley Cooper Attend Taylor Swift's Concert In Paris

It seems Taylor Swift and Travis Kelce have found a new couple to go on double dates with in Gigi Hadid and Bradley Cooper. The model and actor were spotted having the time of their lives at Taylor Swift's concert in Paris, one of the first of the singer's European leg of her Eras tour.

But they weren't alone. Travis Kelce also attended that show, as he had promised a little while before the tour began. He had promised he would accompany Taylor on tour to support her, and that's just what he did. If he got to have a blast listening to great music with his celebrity friends, well, that's just an added bonus.

A new viral video came out that showed Gigi, Bradley Cooper, and Travis, dancing together to Taylor Swift's hit "Blank Space". They were in their private box, and Gigi Hadid looked ecstatic as she waved her arms around, right in between her boyfriend and the Kansas City Chiefs player, who were swaying to the rhythm as well.
