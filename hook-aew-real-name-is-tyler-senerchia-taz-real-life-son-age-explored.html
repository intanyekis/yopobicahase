Hook AEW Real Name Is Tyler Senerchia

Though many know him as Hook, his given name is Tyler Senerchia. His father called his first match, no less, which made it all the more special. 

He is not very active on Instagram, with only 11 posts since his first post on March 13, 2020.

In his first wrestling match as a professional, he won, making his fans and family proud. He used the Tazmission move to force his opponent into submission. The move was made famous by his father, Taz.  

Taz Real Life Son Age Explored

The age of Taz’s son is 22 at the moment of debut. Taz is a wrestling legend who is currently enjoying his time as a commentator of AEW. 

His father expressed his immense joy at getting to “call his first worldwide TV match.” Taz made his feelings known to all on Twitter as he said how much proud he and his wife are of their son.

They had always been very supportive of him and were always proud not only as a student-athlete but also as a person. “He just keeps exceeding our exceptions with his work ethic & focus.”

Insights On Hook As Wrestler

The new and upcoming star started in AEW last year as the latest member of Team Taz. It was announced in an episode of Dynamite in December. 

Since his enlistment, he has trained with the All Elite Wrestling Stars, QT Marshall, and Cody Rhodes, as mentioned by The Sportster. 

QT Marshall discussed Hook’s training and his past as a student-athlete in an interview with Stephanie Chase, the AEW YouTuber and interviewer.

In his words, he said, “He’s a college lacrosse player, so he’s an athlete. Growing up, with his father in the industry, it falls into place.”
