Yachts have always been an ultimate commodity. There is no better way to show off the elite status than owning or vacationing on a classic yacht. Countless celebrities and business people worldwide are yacht enthusiasts who use them as their ultimate luxury transportation to get a much-deserved break and privacy. Celebrities don’t settle by relaxing on a simple yacht. They need the luxury and amenities offered by the best. One of the celebrity favorites is Axioma, a superyacht that provides the best experience possible. The clear and elegant exterior and even more lavish interior oozes luxury from every direction of the yacht.

Initially named Red Square, Axioma is a motor yacht that provides a safe retreat for renters to leisure in style. It features four outdoor locations that seamlessly submerge into one. Equipped with a top-of-the-line crew and numerous luxurious for relaxation, let’s take a look inside this outstanding mega yacht.

Designed To Perfection

Axioma, the 236-foot superyacht, was built in 2013 by Dunya Yachts, and it’s the largest yacht built by the company. It was first displayed at the Monaco Yacht Show as Red Square and received a rave response from the onlookers. The owner of the yacht is a Russian businessman Dmitry Pumpyanskiy. It is created with expert craftsmanship, materials, and engineering to produce a top-notch water transport. Diana and Sterling Scott designed the exterior of the water transport.

Late photographer and interior designer Alberto Pinto decorated the superyacht before his passing in 2012. Pinto always took inspiration from cultural motifs and used the same principle for the yacht. He gave the yacht a beach house-style interior combined with glamorous hotel-inspired designs. Pinto dedicated the design to create a summertime feel and used light-colored woods and straws, and leather as natural fabrics to decorate the ample space, as noted by Yacht Charter Fleet.

Axioma has an ultra-modern stabilizing system that works at zero speeds and rocky waves to reduce roll motion for performance and range. This way, guests can have an effortless cruising journey without experiencing seasickness. The superyacht has a cruising speed of 14 knots with a top speed of 17 knots. It has a range of 6,000 nautical miles with 172,400-liter fuel tanks for a long journey.

The Best In The Class

Axioma has a myriad of amenities and keeps it limited to a few guests. As mentioned by Super Yacht Fan, the superyacht can accommodate 12 guests and 20 crew members. It has five exteriors, each featuring gorgeous views of the blue lagoons. The master suite is designed in neutral colors and has wide windows with a circular skylight for basking in the sunlight. There are additional five staterooms: a VIP suite, two double rooms, and two convertible twin cabins. All the rooms are located on the main deck and are designed with a specific color scheme.

As stated by Boat International, the superyacht features a bar, a spacious salon, a gym, and a cinema room on the lower deck for a theatre-like experience. The sun deck has ample space to host a party using the barbecue station on board. For wellness and relaxation, Axioma has a steam room and sauna on the bridge deck, accessible to all guests from every direction. One of the unique features of the boat is the double-deck gallery that is created by combing two decks so guests can have easy access to the bar and lounge areas.

A Celebrity Favorite

A humongous superyacht that features amenities, good lighting, and helpful staff. Axioma checks off the celebrity requirements for every vacation. The company also provides essential toys with the yacht, such as paddleboards, kayaks, inflatable slides, and diving equipment. The RYA sports center approves Axioma so that guests can get their Jet Ski certification on the luxury charter.

With the help of these amenities, celebrities usually turn the Mediterranean Sea into their private playground. The inflatable slide can be directly connected from the sun deck into the water for a dive down into the sea. In 2015, Kendall Jenner was spotted enjoying the Axioma experience in Monaco with her friends Gigi Hadid, Bella Hadid, Hailey Baldwin, and her then-boyfriend Lewis Hamilton. According to The Daily Mail, the best friends had vacationed on the yacht following the Monte Carlo Grand Prix. The celebrities were spotted jet skiing, diving, and cruising down the inflatable pool into the luminous water.

Axioma the superyacht has become a celebrity favorite and is still chartered around for relaxing vacations by elite families. It features an experienced crew of 20 people that will be available any time of the day to help, and the amenities speak for themselves. The yacht is available for seasonal charter, and it has a weekly rate of $523,500 for the summers and $343,000 for the winters.
