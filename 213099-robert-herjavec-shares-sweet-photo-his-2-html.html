Robert Herjavec's Baby Daughter Haven Mae Smiles Alongside Dad in a Sweet Photo

 Posted on July 14, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  556 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

Canadian businessman and television personality Robert Herjevac took to his Instagram page to share a cute picture of his 2-year-old daughter, Haven Mae, showing how much the little tot has grown.

Robert Herjevac is a doting father and showed that side of him with a recent picture he shared on his Instagram page. The photo showed the proud father enjoying a moment with his little daughter, Haven Mae, albeit in the trunk of his vehicle.

Herjevac and Mae sat on the trunk with the little tot wearing a purple sweatshirt, with a handkerchief tied around her neck. The 2-year-old matched her look with colorful pants and sneakers, while also holding onto a bunny doll.

Kym Herjavec and Robert Herjavec attend the Television Industry Advocacy Awards on September 16, 2016 | Photo: Getty Images

Herjevac, on the other hand, wore a white long-sleeved buttoned shirt, which he tucked into a jean. He also wore dark shades and held a cup of coffee, with another cup on a tray that was also on the trunk.

It looked as though Mae must have said or done something funny to make her father laugh out so loud when the photo was taken. In his caption to the post, Herjevac revealed that he was having his first tailgate party with his special daughter, writing:

"Who said you need sports to tailgate? What better way to experience my first tailgate than to try it with my special girl...Daddy Daughter Tailgate party!"

Herjevac, a star of "Shark Tank," looked happier than ever while staring into his adorable daughter's eyes in the picture, enjoying some fresh air and the scenery around them.

The television host is never shy to show off his affection for his kids and his pride at being a father on social media. He shares Mae and her twin brother Hudson, with his wife, Kym Johnson.

The 57-year-old Herjevac was previously married to Diane Plese from 1990 to 2016 when their divorce was finalized

In April, the twins celebrated their second birthday in quarantine amid the COVID-19 pandemic. Herjevac made the day special with a sweet post in tribute to his lovely children on his Instagram page.

In the post, Herjevac shared videos and pictures from the celebration, showing that it was fun-filled despite the pandemic, with some neighbors arriving in their cars to honor the twins. He captioned the post:

'Happy birthday, Hudson and Haven – the big 2! And thank you to our amazing neighbors for coming together to throw a parade !"

Kym Johnson and Robert Herjevac fell in love during the 2015 rehearsals of "Dancing With the Stars." After their elimination in the 8th week, the pair continued seeing each other, leading into their marriage.

They married in the summer of 2016, saying their vows in front of their friends and family members in Los Angeles. "DWTS" alumni such as Joey Fatone, Donny Osmond, and Lance Bass, were also in attendance.

The 57-year-old Herjevac was previously married to Diane Plese from 1990 to 2016, and their divorce was finalized, two years after their separation in 2014. With Plese, he shares three children, daughters, Caprice and Sky, and son, Brendan.

The Canadian businessman was born on September 14, 1962. He is known for having built and sold several I.T companies, and is a globally renowned cybersecurity operations leader.
