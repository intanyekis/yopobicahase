What voices does Alex Borstein do on Family Guy?

 Posted on June 5, 2024  |  1 minutes  |  160 words  |  Larita Shotwell

Alex Borstein is an American actress, comedian, writer and producer. Alex Borstein is the voice actress of Lois Griffin on the animated comedy television series Family Guy since 1999.

Aside from being the voice actress for Lois Griffin, Alex Borstein also voices Asian correspondent Tricia Takanawa, Loretta Brown and Lois’ mother Barbara Pewterschmidt.

Aside from her work on Family Guy, Borstein also played various characters on the sketch comedy series MADtv (1997-2009) and Dawn Forchette in the medical comedy series Getting On (2013–2015).

She appeared in a number of films as a supporting actress, including The Lizzie McGuire Movie, Catwoman (2004), Good Night, and Good Luck (2005), Dinner for Schmucks (2010), Ted (2012), ParaNorman (2012), and A Million Ways to Die in the West (2014).

Alex Borstein was also a writer and voice actor for several television shows, including Casper, Pinky and the Brain, and Power Rangers Zeo before joining the cast of MADtv in 1997 as a featured player.
