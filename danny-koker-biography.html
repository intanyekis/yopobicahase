Who is Danny Koker?

Danny Koker is an American television personality. People mostly know him for his appearance in the reality television series, ‘Counting Cars’.

Additionally, he is also the creative force and producer of the show. He loves cars and motorcycles so much that he is also called a car- and bike-buying machine.

Danny Koker: Age, Parents, Ethnicity

Koker was born in Cleveland, Ohio on January 5, 1964, as Daniel Nicholas Koker II. He was born to parents Mary Koker and Danny Koker.

In addition, he developed an interest in automobiles since his childhood years. He is of American nationality. Furthermore, his ethnicity is Caucasian.

Talking about his education, Koker attended Valley High School. Furthermore, he also went to the University of Nevada.

Danny Koker: Career, Professional life

Danny Koker is mostly recognized for his appearance in the TV series ‘Counting Cars’. Additionally, he has also worked as the producer for the show. Previously, he appeared in another TV series, ‘Pawn Stars.

Furthermore, Danny has also appeared in ‘The Weekend in Vegas’, ‘American Restoration’, ‘Late Show with David Letterman’, ‘Criss Angel Mindfreak’, and ‘Saturday Fright at the Movies.

Apart from his work in television, Koker is also the leader of a hard rock band, ‘Count’s 77’.

The band includes members like Paul Disibio, Tommy Paris, Barry Barnes, John Zito, and Stoney Curtis. On 29th April 2014, the band launched its debut album with Shrapnel Records.

In addition, Danny Koker is the owner of the Count’s Tattoo Company establishment in Las Vegas, Nevada. Furthermore, he is also the owner of the Count’s Kustoms auto repair business in Las Vegas.
