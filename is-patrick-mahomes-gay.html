Who Is Patrick Mahomes? - Patrick Mahomes Wikipedia

Patrick Mahomes Wikipedia: - Patrick Mahomes was born on September 17, 1995, in Tyler, Texas, U.S. Patrick Mahomes is an American football quarterback for the Kansas City Chiefs of the National Football League (NFL). He attended Texas Tech and played football and baseball as the son of former Major League Baseball (MLB) pitcher Pat Mahomes. He gave up baseball after his sophomore year to focus exclusively on football. He topped all NCAA Division I FBS players in numerous categories during his junior year, including passing yards (5,052) and total touchdowns (53). The Kansas City Chiefs picked him 10th overall in the 2017 NFL Draft.

Mahomes spent his debut season as Alex Smith's backup. After the Chiefs traded Smith to the Washington Redskins, he was named the starting in 2018. Mahomes passed for 5,097 yards, 50 touchdowns, and 12 interceptions that season. He became the only quarterback in history to pass for more than 5,000 yards in both a collegiate and an NFL season.
