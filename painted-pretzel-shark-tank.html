    The Painted Pretzel on Shark Tank – Deal Highlights

    When Raven Thomas appeared on Shark Tank, she sought a $100,000 investment for a 25% equity stake in The Painted Pretzel, valuing the company at $400,000. The Sharks were impressed by the taste and presentation of the pretzels and the potential for scalability. Ultimately, Mark Cuban saw the potential in The Painted Pretzel and offered the desired deal. He not only invested $100,000 but also proposed that Raven relocate her business to Dallas, Texas, to position it for further growth. The partnership with Mark Cuban brought exposure, guidance, and opportunities for expansion.

    “I saw the potential in The Painted Pretzel and the unique offering it brings to the market. The combination of delicious flavors and visually appealing presentation made it a winning proposition,” said Mark Cuban during the episode.

    “We don’t just see pretzels. What we see is the way you are presenting it and the way you are positioning and branding it. You’ve made gourmet pretzels. Our job now—using my picture behind me—is to make sure every American gets to try them.”
