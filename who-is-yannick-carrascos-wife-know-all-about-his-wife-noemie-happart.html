Yannick Carrasco is a professional football player from Belgium who presently plays as a winger for Atlético Madrid. After joining Monaco in 2012, Carrasco rapidly made a name for himself as a gifted young player. He spent his three years at the club helping Monaco win the Ligue 2 title in 2012–2013 and gain promotion to the Ligue 1 league. During his final season in Monaco, Carrasco was an important part of the team that played in the UEFA Champions League.

Carrasco went to Atletico Madrid in 2015. He scored against Marseille in the final game to help Atletico Madrid win the 2017–18 UEFA Europa League. Also, he contributed to his team’s LaLiga triumph in the 2020–21 campaign. Carrasco has earned a regular spot on the Belgium national team as a result of his success with Atletico Madrid. He made his Belgian debut in 2015, and he competed for the third-place match in the 2018 FIFA World Cup. Since then, Carrasco has established himself as a consistent part of the Belgium team and has contributed significantly in the team’s success.

Carrasco joined the Chinese Super League club Dalian Yifang in 2018, where he spent two seasons. He returned to Atletico Madrid on loan in January 2020. Carrasco has had a successful career overall, becoming one of the top wingers in the Spanish league and winning several titles. He is famous for his speed, dribbling skills, and versatility on the pitch in addition to being able to play on both sides and as an offensive midfielder. Because of his work with Atletico Madrid, he has also established himself as a big-game player who can perform under pressure.
