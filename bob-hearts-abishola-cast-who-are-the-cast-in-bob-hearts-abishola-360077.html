Bob Hearts Abishola

Bob Hearts Abishola is sitcom TV series. It is an American series created by  Eddie Gorodetsky, Chuck Lorre, Gina Yashere, and Al Higgins. This series was telecasted on 23rd September 2019 on CBS.  This movie stars Folake Olowofoyeku and Billy Gardell as the lead characters. Bob Hearts Abishola Plot

Bob Wheeler is running his family successfully.  He lives with his mother Dottie and his younger twin siblings Douglas and Christina. The stress in his job makes Bob admitted to Woodward Memorial Hospital. Bob gets a mild heart attack. He is attracted by the young and hard-working nurse Abishola Adebambo.
