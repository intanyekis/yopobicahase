JJ Garcia is one of those guys who keeps doing his thing under the radar. The American businessman works as a consultant and recruiter and is quite successful at it. Even though Garcia dislikes fame and does his best to stay away from media, his sisters are the exact opposite. JJ Garcia is the only brother of the Bella sisters, Nikki Bella and Brie Bella. Both Nikki and Brie are renowned figures in the WWE universe. Garcia has also made a brief appearance in the reality series, Total Bellas. His appearance lasted for a total of two episodes and he hasn’t been seen ever since. In this article, we take a closer look at the wiki-bio of JJ Garcia. 

Wiki/Bio Facts

Early Life

JJ Garcia was born as Jonathan Edward Garcia Jr. on the 31st of March 1989 in San Diego, California. Born to Kathy Laurinaitis and Jon Garcia, JJ is the eldest son of the family. He is also the elder brother of twin sisters Nikki and Brie. JJ spent much of his early life with his family and was into soccer like his sisters. JJ attended a local high school and graduated in 2007. He later enrolled at the Gilbert Community College in Arizona and graduated in 2011.  

Professional Career and Net Worth

JJ has not revealed bits and snippets about his early career. All we know is that he works as an executive search consultant for JBN & Associates, LLC. The company operates out of Arizona and JJ has been associated with the company for quite a while. Back in his early years, JJ was quite the soccer player and wanted to make a career out of it. But he later abandoned his dream and set realistic goals for himself. His career as an executive net JJ a considerable sum of money.  

As of 2023, his net worth is estimated to be around USD 1 million.

Total Bellas

Even though JJ shied away from fame, his sisters enjoyed it. Their career in WWE brought more media attention to the family and the sisters started working on a reality series. Entitled, Total Bellas, the series featured the Bella sisters away from all the glamor of WWE. When the sisters first pitched the idea to JJ he said that the whole thing would blow upon their faces. His disdain towards social media was clear in his statement when he remarked,’ Social media and reporters are preying on people, waiting for someone to say something controversial’. Contrary to JJ’s beliefs the show didn’t blow up and the sisters convinced him to appear on the series.  

Personal Life: Still married or divorced?

JJ Garcia is currently separated from Lauren after 10 years of marriage. She announced their split on Instagram on July 20, 2021, saying that there has been a “shift” in their lives in the past few months and that they are asking for privacy and respect. JJ Garcia has not publicly commented on their separation.

JJ Garcia and Lauren Garcia got married on April 23, 2011, and have two daughters together: Vivienne James Garcia (born on October 21, 2016) and Alice Jude Garcia (born on August 30, 2018). The pair also adopted a baby boy, Jake Ethan Garcia. They celebrated their 10-year wedding anniversary in April 2021, with JJ Garcia posting a loving message to his wife on Instagram. However, it seems that their relationship has deteriorated since then, as they have decided to part ways.

As of 2023, JJ Garcia has not announced publicly whether he is in a relationship or divorced.
