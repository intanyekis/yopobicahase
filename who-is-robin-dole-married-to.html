Robin Dole is a mother of two children and a married woman. She is best recognized for being the daughter of the late Senator Bob Dole. 

Bob Dole, the former Republican Senate Leader, died on Sunday, December 5, 2021, at 98.

Dole was also a US senator from Kansas and the Republican presidential contender in 1996 when he ran against Democrat Bill Clinton. In the 1976 presidential election, Bob was also a vice-presidential contender.

Who Is Robin Dole Married To? Her Husband Name Revealed 

Robin Dole has remained tight-lipped about her husband's whereabouts.

However, her Instagram bio (@robinldole) states that she is the mother of twins. She is most likely married at the age of 67. Nonetheless, her husband's name, as well as the names of her children, remain unknown.

It's difficult to speculate about Robin's personal life because she isn't active on social media.

Although nothing is known about her spouse and children, we know a few things about her family. Gloria Dole Nelson and Norma Steele are her aunts. Her late mother's name is Phyllis Holden, and her stepmother's name is Elizabeth Dole.

Meet Robin Dole Parents Phyllis & Bob Dole

Robin Dole's father, Bob Dole, is a former American politician. 

Dole married Robin's mother, Phyllis Holden, an occupational therapist at a veterans hospital, three months after they met in Battle Creek, Michigan, in 1948.

On October 15, 1954, their daughter, Robin, was born. On January 11, 1972, Dole and Holden divorced. On April 22, 2008, Holden passed away.

In 1972, Dole met his second wife, Elizabeth. On December 6, 1975, the pair married. They didn't have any children.

Dole was a Russell Lodge No. 177 in Russell, Kansas, and was a Freemason. Dole was raised to the Scottish Rite's 33rd degree in 1975.

In 1991, Dole underwent prostate cancer surgery. Later, he testified before Congress and made public service announcements on early cancer detection and the erectile dysfunction he experienced due to his surgery.

He then became a paid Viagra spokesperson. He also starred in a Pepsi commercial parody of his Viagra commercials.

All about Bob Dole Daughter Robin Dole 

Robin, Dole's child with his first wife, Phyllis Holden, is his only daughter. Robin Dole has remained relatively unknown and unnoticed despite her father's well-known public persona.

Robin has been an essential presence in her father's life, having spent practically her entire childhood in the Washington region alongside her father.
