Haylie Duff is an American actor, singer, songwriter, voice actor, producer, television host, writer, and fashion designer. She is known for playing Sandy Jameson in the television family drama series 7th Heaven, Elle in the Western CBS TV miniseries True Women, Stevie Marx in the fantasy family drama series Joan of Arcadia, Summer Wheatley in the animated sitcom Napoleon Dynamite, and Allison in the sitcom Real Rob. Haylie has also been cast in a number of films such as The Lizzie McGuire Movie, Addams Family Reunion, My Nanny’s Secret, In Search of Santa, Hope, Slightly Single in L.A., The Adventures of Panda Warrior, The Sandman, Muffin Top: A Love Story, Material Girls, Love Takes Wing, Holiday Engagement, Love Finds a Home, Sweet Surrender, The Bad Twin, Naughty and Nice, Hats Off to Christmas!, All About Christmas Eve, Christmas Belle, Deadly Delusion, Fear Island, and A Belle for Christmas.
