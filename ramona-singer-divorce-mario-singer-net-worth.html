Ramona Singer is an OG The Real Housewives of New York City cast member and fans have seen a lot of her life. While Ramona has been involved in some difficult fights and has said some things that no one would agree with, fans have also seen her raise her daughter Avery and enjoy her marriage to Mario. It was huge news when Ramona shared that she and Mario would be splitting up, and while they have been divorced for several years now, Mario has popped up on the reality show from time to time.

There has been talk that Ramona will be fired from RHONY, and according to Radar Online, season 14 is being cast. While fans wait to see what this next season will look like, fans are also curious about Ramona's relationship with her ex-husband Mario. Ramona has been super successful in her career and Mario has as well. Who has a higher net worth, Ramona or Mario Singer? Let's take a look.

What Are Ramona And Mario Singer's Net Worths?

Before she began appearing on reality TV regularly, Ramona Singer's job included running her business RMS Fashions, Inc.

Ramona Singer has an $18 million net worth, according to Celebrity Net Worth, and the publication says that the star is paid $500,000 for every episode of The Real Housewives of New York City. Ramona has made a lot of money working steadily and also starring on her reality show.

As it turns out, Ramona Singer's ex-husband Mario Singer also has a net worth of $18 million.

In 2005, the couple started a company together called True Faith Jewelry. Mario is the Vice President of Frederick Singer & Sons and Classic Medallics.

It seems like Ramona Singer has always enjoyed working and making sure that she would be okay. Fans know that she had a very sad childhood and as Ramona told Reality Tea, "I have been on my own since 19 years old, and put myself through college.”

According to CBS News, Ramona got a job at Macy's she was a college student back in the '80s. After becoming an assistant sales manager, she entered the management training program. Ramona was able to start RMS Fashions when she was 30 years old because she borrowed money from her dad and began buying clothing. Ramona explained that when she was told that she could pay $10 for 10,000 clothing units, she said that she would pay $7 in cash.

Ramona said, "I'd cold call to gain new clients. When I heard that Leslie Fay was closing, I'd run over and buy 20,000 dresses. You have to have the personality for it. I love negotiating. I think women need to pick up their math skills."

Why Did Ramona And Mario Singer Get Divorced?

Fans of RHONY recall the sad and tough moment when Ramona shared that she and Mario would be breaking up.

Ramona Singer wrote about her divorce in her memoir Life on the Ramona Coaster. According to Bravotv.com, she shared that she could tell that Mario was cheating on her and that she could tell that he was talking to a woman on the phone.

Ramona wrote, "something in my brain just snapped. It was like a veil had been lifted and it dawned on me that he had not been talking on the phone to a different Alan, or to any man for that matter. I stood up and I looked at him. “Mario, that wasn’t a different Alan on the phone, was it? You were talking to a girl just now, weren’t you?” I remember his face looked like he had seen a ghost. His eyes went wide."

The couple got married in 1992 and decided to get divorced in 2013, according to Us Weekly. In 2010, Ramona and Mario renewed their vows, which was part of season 3 of The Real Housewives of New York City.

Mario was seeing Kasey Dexter while he was still married to Ramona. Kasey told Reality Blurb, “I’m very apologetic and sincere. I know I come across that I don’t care but I do. I just try to make light of it even though it’s not." Kasey and Mario broke up when Kasey cheated on Mario."
